Discover Jodhpur

The magnificent Blue City of Rajasthan offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and adventure.

Mehrangarh Fort

One of the largest forts in India, standing 400 feet above the city with impressive views and architecture dating back to 1459.

Open: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

Umaid Bhawan Palace

Part royal residence, part hotel, and part museum, this magnificent palace is one of the world's largest private residences.

Open: 10:00 AM - 4:30 PM

Blue City

Explore the old city with its iconic blue-painted houses that give Jodhpur its famous nickname "The Blue City".
Best View: Climb up to Mehrangarh Fort for a breathtaking panoramic view of the Blue City below.

Best viewed from Mehrangarh Fort
